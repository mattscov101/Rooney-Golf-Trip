Oglebay Courses

Arnold Palmer Course

Par 72 (6,065 yards)

Championship Bentgrass

Location of the iconic match winning putt on 18 between the Cottril's in 2022. Arnold Palmer's signature course featuring rolling fairways, strategic bunkering, and challenging greens that demand precision.

Crispin Course

Par 71 (5,384 yards)

Municipal Bentgrass

The Cripsin course plays to the strengths of the accurate golfers and short game wizards. Maintaining the scenic views and lob-sided stances this course takes the edge of distance but no less of challenge.

Robert Trent Jones Sr. Course

Par 71 (6,046 yards)

Municipal Bentgrass

The Jones course remarkably tricky. Drive off with the hardest hole on the course in front of crowd. This will be a test of each players endurance and ability to keep their game together on the final day.
