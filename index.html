Tyler Zoner

Captain
As captain of Team Zoner in the Bald Man Classic, Tyler is elite at being bald. With his throwback golf aesthetic, broad shoulders, and collection of retro golf attire, he often looks like he should be on the cover of Golf Digest, in 1992. His driver remains a weapon and his wealth of golf knowledge and experience gives him an edge in match-play situations. The only concern is that various parts of his body have recently begun submitting retirement paperwork. If Zoner can stay healthy for the weekend, he'll be a tough out. If not, he'll still make a hell of a breakfast burrito.

Max King

Handicap: 1

Every golf trip has one guy the rest of the field is secretly hoping has an off day. For this group, that's Max. Armed with a T-Town golf pedigree and an elite iron game, he can dismantle a course in a hurry when things are clicking. The good news for everyone else is that his putter occasionally goes into witness protection, and his brain occasionally loses focus after 20 beers. Max has a knack of kicking your ass while simultaneously making sure your round is extremely enjoyable. Known for his late Friday nights, sleeping on couches when beds are available, and exceptional hungover golf, he has quite a reputation to uphold.

Mark Gauer

Handicap: 10

Mark possesses a rare combination of traits in the Rooney Golf Trip field: the strength to bench 400 pounds and the conviction to putt everything within 30 yards of the green. A true power player, Gauer can absolutely mash the golf ball and turn long par 4s and par 5s into scoring opportunities. The challenge has always been consistency, as the spectacular shot is often followed by one that leaves teammates searching for answers. Though his game is hit or miss, Mark's greatest asset may be his positive attitude. Win or lose, he's always ready to hang with the boys.

Pat Rooney

Handicap: 2

As founder, commissioner, and namesake of the Rooney Golf Trip, Pat has spent the last six years proving that good things happen when you combine golf, beers, the boys, and planning trips 8 months out. His game lacks the distance of some of the field's heavy hitters, but few players are more reliable when the pressure is on. Armed with a tidy short game and a knack for draining clutch putts, Pat has built a reputation for turning "boring golf" into winning golf. His biggest weakness is probably being too nice to his opponents, and also not winning his own golf trip in the last 5 years.

Few topics in Rooney Golf Trip history have generated more debate than Billman's handicap. A bomber off the tee with a dangerous wedge game, Ryan has all the tools to dominate a round. Dan Cottrill

Handicap: 6

An original Rooney Golf Trip captain, Dan has spent years chasing golf's greatest prize: finally figuring it out. A talented player with plenty of power, Dan can stripe a driving iron with the best of them and has been a fixture in some of the event's biggest moments. The problem is that every promising round is only one YouTube swing tip away from a complete overhaul. Dan can get into his opponents head with long drives, towering irons, and making plenty of birdies. But he can also get into his own head with a bad bounce, a bad swing, or showing too much knee.

Captain
As captain of Team Shepard, Josh enters the weekend with the burden of leadership and the confidence of a man who has played golf on multiple continents. Bryant Bigler

Handicap: 12

Bigs remains one of the most beloved figures in Rooney Golf Trip history. While some players spend the year grinding on their games, Bryant's annual golf preparation consists primarily of showing up. Despite playing only a handful of rounds each year, he's an exceptional teammate whose enthusiasm for good shots, especially other people's, never wavers. His game is built around a surprisingly reliable half swing and an admirable refusal to get rattled. Every year, Bigs finds a way to steal a point that nobody saw coming. He may have a self-described "lack of bloodlust," but he makes up for it with a surplus of chill vibes.

Cory Cottrill

Handicap: 5

The reigning MVP returns looking to accomplish what many thought impossible: defend his title while playing on one leg. Just a few months removed from a torn Achilles, Cory will arrive equipped with an Easy-Walk peg leg, a handicapped handicap, and all the confidence of a man who has never once questioned his own "Cory logic."
